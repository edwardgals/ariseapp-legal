Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Your morning is private. We built Arise around that principle from day one — and we intend to keep it that way.

Who We Are

Arise is a morning gratitude alarm app developed by Arise App ("we", "us", or "our"). By using Arise, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

This policy applies to all versions of the Arise app available on iOS and Android.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve the Arise experience:

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account
  • User ID — a unique identifier (Firebase UID) to associate your data with your account
  • Alarm settings — your alarm times, repeat days, labels, and preferences
  • Gratitude streak data — your daily completion records and streak count
  • Subscription status — your plan type (Individual or Family) and trial status
  • Push notification token — to deliver your morning alarms to your device
We do not collect your name, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any sensitive personal information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Create and maintain your Arise account
  • Deliver your morning gratitude alarms
  • Sync your alarm settings and streak data across devices
  • Enable Family Plan features including shared dashboards and nudge notifications
  • Manage your subscription and trial period
  • Send push notifications for alarms and family nudges
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the app

Family Plan Data

If you subscribe to the Family Plan, the following additional data is shared within your family group:

  • Each member's email address (visible to the family plan owner)
  • Each member's next alarm time and streak count
  • Each member's daily gratitude completion status

Family members can only see each other's data within their shared family group. No family data is visible to anyone outside the group.

Data Storage & Security

All Arise data is stored securely in Firebase — Google's cloud platform — using industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

We follow best practices for access control and security. Your data is accessible only to you (and your family group members, if applicable).

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. We do not show ads in Arise.

Third-Party Services

Arise uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and push notifications. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for subscription billing and app distribution
  • Expo — for push notification delivery infrastructure

We do not use advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or analytics tools that profile your behavior.

Microphone & Voice Dismissal

Arise's signature feature lets you dismiss your alarm by reading the day's gratitude aloud. To make this possible, the app needs access to your device's microphone and the operating system's speech recognition capability when an alarm is ringing.

Here is exactly what happens with that audio:

  • The microphone is only activated while an alarm is ringing and the voice-dismissal screen is open
  • Speech recognition runs on your device using Apple's or Google's built-in speech APIs
  • We never record, store, transmit, or upload your voice or the speech transcript
  • The only thing the app keeps is whether the dismissal succeeded — not what was said or how it sounded
  • You can disable voice dismissal for any alarm in the alarm settings; the alarm can then be dismissed with a regular tap
Your voice never leaves your device. Speech recognition is processed locally by the operating system. We do not have access to your audio.

The microphone permission can be revoked at any time in your device's system settings (Settings → Arise → Microphone on iOS, or Settings → Apps → Arise → Permissions on Android).

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

Arise is appropriate for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

The Family Plan is the only context in which Arise is intended to be used by minors. In that case:

  • The parent or legal guardian creates and owns the family group
  • The parent invites each family member by email and assigns their role (parent, child, partner, other)
  • By inviting a child, the parent provides verifiable consent on behalf of that child to the data practices described in this policy
  • The parent can remove any family member, including a child, at any time, which deletes that member's data
  • For children under 13, the parent must set up and manage the child's account; the child should not create their own account

If you believe a child has provided personal information to Arise without parental consent, please contact us at privacy@arisapp.com and we will remove it promptly.

  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting the app
  • Manage your subscription in your device's App Store settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@arisapp.com.
