Delete your account

We're sorry to see you go.

You can permanently delete your Arise account and all associated data by submitting the form below — no app required.

What gets deleted

When we process your deletion request, the following is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days:

  • Your Arise account and email address
  • All of your alarm settings, labels, and schedules
  • Your daily gratitude completion records and streak history
  • Your subscription state inside Arise
  • Your push notification token

What we're not able to delete from here

  • Apple App Store / Google Play subscription billing — cancel separately in your device's subscription settings
  • Email correspondence you've sent to us (kept for support and legal records)
This is permanent. Once your account is deleted we cannot restore it, your alarms, or your streak. You'll need to sign up again from scratch if you change your mind.
Faster option

Still have the app installed?

You can delete your account instantly from inside Arise: Profile tab → Settings → Delete account. The form below is for users who no longer have access to the app.

Request deletion

Fill in your details and we'll process your deletion within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email when it's complete.
